About the Temple

The Hong Kong 澄淨堂 (CCT Buddhist Society) is a spiritual sanctuary devoted to the practices of compassion, wisdom, and mindfulness. Our space honors traditional Buddhist rituals and invites visitors of all backgrounds to share in prayer, light offerings, Dharma talks, and charitable events.
